About Us

Enthusiastic, highly motivated and enjoys learning skills, new professional knowledge and well adapted to various working conditions and team environment. Having good skills in various laparoscopic and open surgeries. Additionally, best academics and the core scientific apprehension helps to publish various research article and case study in nation and international journals as well as forums.

In spite of having good hands in basic laparoscopic procedures like laparoscopic cholecystectomy and appendectomy, have done various advanced laparoscopic surgeries independently in hernias like numerous complicated laparoscopic ventral hernioplasties and anterior abdominal wall reconstruction, laparoscopic inguinal hernioplasty (TAPP & TEP) and e- TEP. Even more highly skilled in minimal access surgeries particularly in the field of colorectal surgeries and liver surgeries, laparoscopic small bowel surgeries and perforation closures, laparoscopic gynecological procedures and laparoscopic urological procedures. Moreover, done a variety of advance laparoscopic and open surgeries in GI luminal, oncology and Bariatric surgeries and possess skills in various vascular surgeries such as RFA / LASER for varicose vein surgeries and limb amputations.

Furthermore, skilled in Endoscopic procedure (Upper and Lower GI) both therapeutic and diagnostic like endovenous ligation and stenting in luminal strictures. Other surgical procedures like Modified radical mastectomy for breast cancers and other breast issues, Thyrodectomy for benign and malignant thyroid problems and other hear and neck issues.
